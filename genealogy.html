Genealogy

I inherited our original family history on paper at a Wissmiller reunion some time in the 1980s, and I've been working on Wissmiller genealogy on and off ever since. In general, I'm researching descendants of Jacob C. Wissmiller and Christiana Fink, our original ancesters who settled in central Illinois in the 19th century.

I'm always happy to receive additions or updates of people related in any way (marriage, adoption, etc.).

For births and new people to be added, I need:

  • full name
  • birth date and location
  • any interesting tidbits
  • a portrait style picture, if you have it
For marriages:
  • full names of bride (maiden name) and groom
  • marriage date and location
  • any interesting facts about the marriage
  • a wedding picture, if you have it
For deaths:
  • date and location
  • any further info (obituary, etc.)
Please include some context (to whom the new or updated people are related) so I can fit your update into the existing data.

Also, I'd like to get more pictures of everybody. That makes it more interesting than just names, dates and places. The goal is to have a portrait style picture of as many people as possible.

Web based forms easy data entry:

  • Personal data form
  • Marriage data form

Thanks!

Viewing the Data

Making genealogy data available for others to view is tricky due to privacy concerns. I’ve been storing the data mainly on Ancestry.com. Ancestry and the other main genealogy websites resolve the privacy issue by hiding personal data for all living people. That’s great for privacy but terrible if you want to see your living relatives.

Ancestry allows full access to the person who contributed the data (me), but hides personal information about living persons from others. I may be able to export the data and share it with anyone who is well known to me and has a serious interest in our family's genealogy. If that's you, please contact me.
